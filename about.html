OUR STORY

Welcome To Hotel Sarthi..

At Sarthi, we take immense pride in our rich heritage and longstanding tradition of serving delectable vegetarian cuisine to our esteemed guests. Established many decades ago, our hotel has been an integral part of Kolhapur's cultural and culinary landscape. Over the years, we have garnered a loyal following, drawing patrons from near and far, all seeking the unparalleled flavors and timeless charm we offer.

The Best of Vegetarian Cuisine.

At Sarthi,we believe that pure vegetarian food can be a delightful culinary journey in itself. Our skilled chefs curate an extensive menu, showcasing a variety of traditional and innovative vegetarian dishes, each prepared with the utmost care and using the freshest ingredients sourced from local farmers and markets. Whether it's our signature dishes or regional specialties, every bite encapsulates the essence of our culinary expertise.

Our Hospitality

Beyond the delectable cuisine, our warm and welcoming ambiance ensures that every visit is a memorable one. Our dedicated staff takes great pleasure in providing attentive and personalized service, ensuring that each guest feels right at home. We go the extra mile to ensure your dining experience is nothing short of exceptional.Enjoy a comfortable and enriching stay with our range of modern facilities, thoughtfully designed to cater to all your needs.
